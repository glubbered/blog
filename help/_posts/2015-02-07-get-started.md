Freehand
========

<div class="pure-g">
  <div class="pure-u-1-3">
    <ul>
      <li>On/off toggle F Key.</li>
      <li>Drag to draw.</li>
      <li>Shift + drag draws <strong>straight lines</strong>.</li>
      <li>Double click <strong>closes path</strong>.</li>
